### 1/8 — Hook (가장 중요)

```
i was tired of spending 2 hours every time i wanted to try a new AI tool.

so i built VibeStack — answer 5 questions, get a curated AI stack in
60 seconds.

we just launched on @ProductHunt 🚀

here's the story (and a thread on what we learned) 🧵
```

- 280자 마지노선
- 이미지: PH 런치 페이지 캡처 또는 사이트 hero 캡처
- "thread on what we learned" = 다음 트윗 읽을 이유

### 2/8 — Why

```
the AI tool landscape in 2026:

→ 1000s of tools, 95% identical
→ "best of" articles ranked by who paid
→ comparison spreadsheets that go stale in a week
→ everyone reinventing the same stack

i wanted a friend who'd just tell me: "use these, in this order."
```

### 3/8 — Solution (the quiz)

```
the core of VibeStack is a 5-question quiz:

1. what are you building?
2. solo or team?
3. budget tier?
4. coding ability?
5. workflow preference?

→ output: a hand-picked stack of 3-5 tools that work *together*, with
total cost, learning curve, and why each one was chosen.

60 seconds. free.
```

(이미지: 퀴즈 흐름 GIF 또는 result screen 캡처)

### 4/8 — What's different

```
3 things that make VibeStack different from every other "AI tool
directory":

→ stacks > lists. real combos, not isolated rankings.
→ vetted monthly. no stale "best of 2023" pages.
→ no affiliate ranks. sponsorships labeled clearly.

→ 30+ tools, 6 stacks, 41 deep-dives. growing slowly on purpose.
```

### 5/8 — Stacks (the unique angle)

```
the "stacks" concept is the part i'm most excited about.

examples shipped:
→ 10x Engineer Stack ($40/mo, 2 tools)
→ Product Designer Stack
→ Smart Assistant Stack
→ Power Pair Stack
→ Learner Stack
→ Efficiency Stack

borrow one. fork it. build your own. (coming for pro users.)
```

(이미지: 6개 스택 그리드 캡처 또는 /stack/10x-engineer)

### 6/8 — Receipts (proof)

```
real numbers from VibeStack today:

→ 30+ AI tools, hand-tested

→ 150+ head-to-head comparison pages
```

```
shipping > waiting. v1 first, polish second.
```
