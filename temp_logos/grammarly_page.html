Brand Information

Grammarly is an online grammar and writing tool that helps users to check and improve their writing. It uses advanced algorithms to check for grammar, punctuation, and style errors, as well as provide suggestions for improving readability and clarity. The tool covers a wide range of writing, from academic papers and business documents to emails and social media posts.

Grammarly can be used as a browser extension, a desktop application, or as an integration with other tools such as Microsoft Word, Google Docs and more. It checks grammar and spelling, punctuation, sentence structure, style, and even tone of the text. It suggests improvements and provides explanations for the changes it suggests.One of the key strengths of Grammarly is its ability to adapt to the user's writing style, it can recognize different types of writing and adjust its suggestions accordingly. Additionally, it also offers a plagiarism checker, which checks the text against an extensive database of websites, articles, and other texts.
